❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Do I need to do anything to use this?
A: No! Errors are automatically translated when using the IDE.

Q: What if I see an untranslated error?
A: Rare! But you can add it to errorCodeMappings.ts.

Q: Will this slow down my app?
A: No. Translations happen in < 0.2ms and only when errors occur.

Q: Can I customize error messages?
A: Yes! Edit errorCodeMappings.ts or generateSuggestions() method.

Q: Can I see the original error?
A: Yes! Click "Details" to see the raw error code and message.
